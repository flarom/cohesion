Features

Markdown-Based

Write complex documents without taking your hands out of the keyboard.

In-browser local storage

Your files are not sent to us, Cohesion saves your files in your browser data, you can also create backups of all your data in one single click.

Themes

Personalize your experience with 8 themes. Switch between light and dark modes, or choose from unique palettes to make your writing environment more comfortable and inspiring.

Easy export

Export your notes to formats like PDF, HTML, or Markdown with just one click.

Instant search

Quickly find any note or piece of text using the built-in instant search, streamlining your workflow.

Distraction-free editor

Write without any distractions. You don't have to create an account, sign a monthly subscription, watch ads, or train AI. It's a text editor!
